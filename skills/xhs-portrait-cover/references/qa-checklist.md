# Cover QA Checklist

## Copy

- Headline matches the approved wording character for character.
- English product names and capitalization are correct.
- No extra AI-generated words or duplicated labels appear.
- The title remains readable as a small feed thumbnail.

## Person

- The person is immediately recognizable.
- Face shape, glasses, moles, hairstyle, expression, clothing, and proportions remain intact.
- Skin retains texture and plausible tone.
- Eyes are clear without being enlarged or unnaturally sharpened.

## Lighting

- Face light comes from the camera direction.
- Forehead, both cheeks, eyes, nose, mouth, chin, neck, and chest transition consistently.
- Shadows under glasses are lifted without erasing the glasses.
- No white or blue outline surrounds hair, shoulders, arms, or clothes.
- No clipped highlights, oily shine, gray skin, or globally over-bright background.

## Composition

- Canvas is 3:4 with safe margins.
- Title, portrait, label, and logos have an obvious hierarchy.
- Logos are secondary and not duplicated.
- Brand color acts as an accent unless a full-color background was explicitly requested.
- The original background remains recognizable when requested.

## Output

- No Xiaohongshu interface, username, likes, view count, watermark, or border unless requested.
- No distorted hands, glasses, jewelry, or clothing.
- Revisions changed only the requested variable.
